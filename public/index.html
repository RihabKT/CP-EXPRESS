THE FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION?

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) is the domestic intelligence and security service of the United States
and its principal federal law enforcement agency.
Operating under the jurisdiction of the United States Department of Justice, the FBI is also a member of the U.S.
Intelligence Community and reports to both the Attorney General and the Director of National Intelligence.
A leading U.S. counter-terrorism, counterintelligence, and criminal investigative organization, the FBI has
jurisdiction over violations of more than 200 categories of federal crimes.
The mission of the FBI is: Protect the American people and uphold the Constitution of the United States.
